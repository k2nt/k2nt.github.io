Welcome. This is the first post on my new site, built with [Astro](https://astro.build)
and deployed to GitHub Pages.

I wanted a calm, minimal place to write — somewhere I own, that loads fast and
gets out of the way. No tracking, no clutter, just words.

## What you'll find here

- **Blog** — longer-form writing: things I'm thinking about, building, or figuring out.
- **TIL** — short "today I learned" notes, mostly for my future self.

## Why Astro

It lets me write in plain Markdown, ships almost no JavaScript, and keeps my
content portable. If I ever get bored and switch frameworks, the posts come with me.
